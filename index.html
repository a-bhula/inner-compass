What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

Common Mental Health Issues

Common mental health issues include:

  • Anxiety: Characterized by feelings of worry, nervousness, or fear that are strong enough to interfere with one's daily activities.
  • Depression: A mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest.
  • Bipolar Disorder: A disorder associated with episodes of mood swings ranging from depressive lows to manic highs.
  • Schizophrenia: A serious mental disorder in which people interpret reality abnormally, which may result in hallucinations, delusions, and extremely disordered thinking and behavior.

These conditions can affect anyone and can have a significant impact on daily life.
