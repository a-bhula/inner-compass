what is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Good mental health is essential at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

Mental health problems are common, but help is available. People with mental health problems can get better and many recover completely.

Inner Compass is dedicated to providing resources and support to help individuals navigate their mental health journey. We offer a variety of tools, including self-assessment quizzes, articles, and access to professional help, to empower you to take control of your mental well-being.

common mental health issues

Common mental health issues include:

  • Anxiety: Characterized by feelings of worry, nervousness, or fear that are strong enough to interfere with one's daily activities.
  • Depression: A mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest.
  • Bipolar Disorder: A disorder associated with episodes of mood swings ranging from depressive lows to manic highs.
  • Schizophrenia: A serious mental disorder in which people interpret reality abnormally, which may result in hallucinations, delusions, and extremely disordered thinking and behavior.

These conditions can affect anyone and can have a significant impact on daily life.

mental health tips

Stay Active

Regular physical activity can help improve your mood and reduce anxiety.

Eat Well

A balanced diet can help you feel better and improve your overall health.

Get Enough Sleep

Quality sleep is essential for mental and physical well-being.

Stay Connected

Maintaining relationships with friends and family can provide support and reduce stress.
